Giant rolls

Tuna roll Ham and cheese roll Egg and salami roll
Tuna, mayonnaise, salt, pepper, lettuce,
tomato and a roll		 Lettuce, ham, tomato, cheese, salt, pepper
and a roll		 2 hard boiled eggs, sprouts, salami, salt,
pepper, mayonnaise and a roll

To make the egg and salami rolls, follow these steps:
1. Tap the eggs on the table, then peel the cracked shells. Wash off any bits of shell left on the eggs.
2. In a clean bowl, mash the eggs with a fork. Mix in the sprouts, mayonnaise, salt, and black pepper.
3. On the cutting board, slice a roll in half with a sharp knife. Spread a little butter onto each half.
4. Spread a spoonful of the egg mixture evenly over the bottom half of the roll.
5. Take 3 slices of salami. Fold each in half again. Put this on top of the egg mixture.

Chocolate Chip cookies

Chocolate Chip cookies
8 tablespoons soft butter, 1/3 cup soft brown sugar,
1/3 cup sugar, 1 egg, 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract,
1/2 teaspoon baking soda, 1/4 teaspoon salt,
1 cup all-purpose flour, 1 cup chocolate chips.

To make these amazing cookies, follow these steps:
1. Put the butter and both sugars in the mixing bowl. Beat until soft and creamy.
2. Break the egg into the bowl. Beat the mixture until smooth.
3. Stir in the vanilla extract, flour, salt and baking soda, a little at a time.
4. Add in the chocolate chips and mix well.
5. Put small mounds of the mixture onto the cookie sheet. Leave plenty of space between each cookie.
6. Bake the cookies for 10 to 12 minutes, until they are golden brown.

Peppermint Patties

Peppermint Patties
1 egg white, 3 cups of sugar(powdered), a few drops of
peppermint extract, a few drops of red food colouring and
a few drops of green food coloring.

To make the peppermint patties, follow these steps:
1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk the egg white until it's light and frothy.
2. Stir the sugar into the eggwhite.
3. Using your hands, massage the dough until it's soft and tender.
4. Split the mixture into 3 balls, keep one of them white and the others red and green.
5. For each color; roll the mixture into small balls, and flat them down with a fork.
6. Let them set overnight.
